# TheFreeFix.com — Future Ideas

## Games — Weird & Fun
- **Useless Button** — A button that does increasingly absurd things when clicked (confetti, screen flip, fake error popups, spawns more buttons). Pure dopamine share-bait.
- **Cookie Clicker Lite** — Minimalist idle/clicker. Click a thing, buy upgrades, numbers go brrr. Stupidly addictive.
- **Liar's Dice** — Play against AI opponents. Bluffing game, surprisingly strategic. Almost nobody has this as a browser game.
- **Pipe Dream** — Connect pipes before water flows. Retro puzzle, very buildable in canvas.
- **Tower of Hanoi** — Classic math puzzle with a slick UI. Drag discs, track moves, time it.
- **Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock** — Big Bang Theory variant. Best of 5 vs AI with over-the-top animations.
- **Beetle** — Old dice game where you draw a beetle piece by piece based on rolls. Charming and obscure.

## Tools — Chaotic & Niche
- **Excuse Generator** — "Why I can't come to work" / "Why my homework is late" with absurdity slider from plausible to unhinged. *(BUILDING FIRST)*
- **Corporate Buzzword Generator** — "We need to synergize our blockchain-adjacent AI pipeline." Meeting bingo vibes.
- **Fake Receipt Generator** — Generate obviously fake receipts ("3x Unicorn Tears — $47.00"). Comedy, not fraud.
- **Name My Pet** — Random pet name generator with filters (species, vibe: regal / silly / food-themed).
- **Decision Wheel** — Spin a customizable wheel to decide things. "Where should we eat?" energy.
- **ASCII Art Generator** — Type text, get it in big ASCII block letters. Nostalgia tool.
- **Lorem Ipsum but Weird** — Placeholder text generators: pirate speak, corporate jargon, cat facts, medieval English.

## Notes
- Excuse generator and decision wheel likely get the most organic traffic
- Useless button is pure share-bait / viral potential
- All fit the static HTML/CSS/JS stack — no backend needed
