About

What Is TheFreeFix.com?

TheFreeFix.com is a collection of free browser-based games and tools. No accounts, no downloads, no paywalls — everything runs right in your browser. We believe simple, well-made games and utilities should be accessible to everyone without friction.

What We Offer

We have a growing library of classic games and useful tools:

Games

  • Sudoku — 4 difficulty levels, notes mode, hints, stats tracking
  • Minesweeper — 3 difficulty levels, flagging, chord clicking, best times
  • 2048 — slide and merge tiles, score tracking, best score persistence
  • Word Leap — daily word puzzle, change one letter at a time
  • Block Drop — classic falling-block puzzle with hold and ghost piece
  • Chess Puzzles — solve tactical puzzles, multiple difficulty levels
  • Memory Match — card-matching game with multiple grid sizes
  • Solitaire — classic Klondike solitaire with draw-one and draw-three

Tools

  • QR Code Generator — create QR codes for URLs, text, and more
  • Word Counter — count words, characters, sentences, and reading time
  • PDF Merge & Split — combine or split PDF files in your browser
  • Color Palette Generator — generate beautiful color schemes for designs
  • Image Compressor — compress JPEG, PNG, and WebP images
  • JSON Formatter — format, validate, and minify JSON instantly

New games and tools are added regularly.

Our Approach

Every game and tool on this site is built with a few principles in mind:

  • Free forever — no paywalls, no premium tiers, no hidden costs
  • No sign-ups — play instantly without creating an account
  • Works offline — once loaded, games work without an internet connection
  • Privacy first — we don't collect personal data; game stats stay on your device
  • Mobile friendly — designed to work well on phones, tablets, and desktops

How We're Supported

TheFreeFix.com is supported by non-intrusive advertising. This lets us keep everything free while covering hosting costs. We limit ads to a maximum of two per page and never use pop-ups or overlays that disrupt your experience.
