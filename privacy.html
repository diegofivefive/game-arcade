TheFreeFix.com ("we", "us", or "our") provides free online games and tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit our site and how it is used. We are committed to keeping this simple and transparent.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use this site. The only data stored on your device is saved locally in your browser:

  • Game preferences — difficulty level, theme (dark/light), and settings toggles
  • Game statistics — games played, wins, streaks, and best times

This data is stored using your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to us or any third party. It stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data.

Advertising

We display advertisements to support the site. Our advertising partner(s) may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads that are relevant to you based on your browsing activity across websites.

Ad partners may collect data including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Interactions with ads

To opt out of interest-based advertising, visit aboutads.info or networkadvertising.org.

Cookies

We do not set our own cookies. Our advertising partner(s) may set cookies for ad personalization and measurement. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect ad delivery but will not affect your ability to use the games and tools on this site.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server log data (such as IP addresses) as part of normal web hosting operations. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

DNS and CDN services are provided by Cloudflare. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information ourselves, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. For data collected by our advertising partner(s), please refer to their respective privacy policies. Residents of the EU, UK, and California may have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA — these are handled through our advertising partner's consent mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at hello@thefreefix.com.
