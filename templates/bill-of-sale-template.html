Bill of Sale Template

Document the sale of vehicles, equipment, or personal property with a proper bill of sale. This template creates a clear record of the transaction for both parties.

What's Included

  • Seller name and address
  • Buyer name and address
  • Item description and condition
  • Sale price and payment method
  • Date of sale and governing state
  • Signature lines for both parties
  • Legal disclaimer

Common Uses

  • Selling a used car or vehicle privately
  • Selling equipment, furniture, or electronics
  • Documenting any private sale for tax or legal records
  • Protecting both buyer and seller with a written agreement
