Power of Attorney Template

Authorize someone you trust to act on your behalf in specific matters. This template creates a power of attorney document covering the scope and limitations of authority.

What's Included

  • Principal (grantor) name and address
  • Agent (attorney-in-fact) name and address
  • Scope of authority granted
  • Effective date and governing state
  • Revocation provisions
  • Witness and notary sections
  • Signature lines
  • Legal disclaimer

Common Uses

  • Authorizing someone to handle financial affairs
  • Medical or healthcare decision-making
  • Real estate transactions when you cannot be present
  • Any situation requiring legal delegation of authority
