How to Use the Metadata Stripper

Drag and drop your images or click the upload area to select files. The tool instantly scans each image for hidden metadata including GPS coordinates, camera details, software information, and timestamps. Click "Strip All" to remove metadata from every image at once, or strip files individually. Download cleaned images one by one or all at once as a batch.

Features

Remove EXIF, IPTC, and XMP metadata from JPEG, PNG, and WebP images entirely in your browser. Batch process up to 50 files at once. View a detailed breakdown of detected metadata before stripping. GPS location data is flagged as a privacy warning. All processing happens locally — your images never leave your device.
