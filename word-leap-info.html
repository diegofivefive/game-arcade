How to Play

Guess the secret 5-letter word in 6 tries. After each guess, the tiles change color to show how close you are.

  • Green — the letter is correct and in the right position
  • Yellow — the letter is in the word but in the wrong position
  • Gray — the letter is not in the word at all

Type using your keyboard or tap the on-screen keys. Press Enter to submit a guess and Backspace to delete.

Strategy Tips

  • Start with a word that has common letters like CRANE, SLATE, or AUDIO
  • Use your second guess to test different common letters
  • Pay attention to yellow letters — they tell you a letter is present but needs to move
  • Avoid reusing gray letters in future guesses
  • The keyboard at the bottom tracks which letters you have already tried

About

Word Leap is a 5-letter word puzzle game inspired by the classic word-guessing genre. It features animated tile reveals, a color-coded keyboard that tracks your guesses, statistics with guess distribution, and unlimited plays.

The game includes around 700 carefully curated answer words and accepts over 2,500 valid guesses. All game logic runs in your browser with no server needed. Your stats and streaks are saved automatically.
