How to Play Solitaire Online

Solitaire is a single-player card game collection featuring three popular variants. In Klondike, build foundation piles from ace to king by suit while arranging cards in alternating-color descending sequences on the tableau. Spider Solitaire uses two decks and challenges you to build complete suited sequences. FreeCell gives you four free cells as temporary storage to help sort all cards to the foundations.

Game Features

Play three different solitaire variants in one place: Klondike, Spider, and FreeCell. Each variant has its own rules and strategy. Drag and drop cards or click to move them automatically. Your game statistics including wins, games played, and win rate are saved for each variant. The card layout adapts to your screen size for comfortable play on any device.
